How to Use WiFi QR Codes

For Android Devices:

  1. Open the Camera app
  2. Point camera at the QR code
  3. Tap the WiFi notification that appears
  4. Confirm connection to join the network

For iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open the Camera app
  2. Point camera at the QR code
  3. Tap "Join [Network Name]" notification
  4. Device connects automatically

Security Tips:

  • Only share WiFi QR codes with trusted guests
  • Consider creating a guest network for visitors
  • Use WPA3 security when available for maximum protection
  • Regularly update your WiFi password for security
